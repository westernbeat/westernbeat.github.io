Fast Bricks Robotics

Control Systems Engineer

Who are Fast Bricks Robotics?

Fast bricks Robotics have developed a robot called the Hadrian-X. Which can lay bricks 15 times the size of a standard house brick. The Hadrian-X can also cut the bricks to size, router channels for plumbing and electrical cables and apply the adhesive to bond the bricks together. The Hadrian-x Maximizes production, reduces build times and waste, relieves trades of repetitive work and allowing them to focus on more specialized architectural designs.

The Fast bricks robotics team are developing software called the Architectual designer, which drives the Hadrian-X allowing it to achieve significant performance advantages.

Position Description

Fast bricks robotics are seeking intelligent, curious team players to join their team of lifelong learners who challenge the norm, take risks, are flexible and inquisitive in nature, are motivated and thrive in fast paced environments. The successful applicant will be able to think outside the box and be willing to roll up their sleeves and step outside of their position title when required. Within a close-knit team, the successful applicant will be pivotal in the contribution to design and implementation of projects.
